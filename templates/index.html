The Process

  • Deadline: Submissions are accepted until Sunday evening.
  • Shortlisting: An AI-assisted review identifies the top manuscripts on Monday.
  • Selection: The weekly laureate is selected by our editor from the shortlist.
  • Announcement: The new winner is announced publicly every Thursday.
